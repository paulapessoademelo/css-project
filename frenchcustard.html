FRENCH CUSTARD

Photo by

Ingredients

  • 1 quart milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla

    • Directions

    1. Put the milk on to boil in a farina boiler
    2. Bet the yelks with the sugar and cornstarch, then add them to the boiling milk
    3. Then put the custard into the pudding dish
    4. Make a meringue of the beaten whites of the eggs with 1 tablespoonful of pulverized sugar and a tablespoonful of vanilla
    5. Drop the meringue over the custard, and place it in the oven only long enough to brown the meringue
